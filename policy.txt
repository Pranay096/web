# SmartFish Policy Notes – September 2025 (Expanded Full Version)

------------------------------------------------------------
1. Fisheries Management Goals
------------------------------------------------------------
- Sustainable exploitation of marine & inland stocks
- Reduce juvenile and illegal catches
- Protect endangered species
- Support small-scale fishers economically
- Ensure equitable trade and mandi pricing
- Encourage cooperative fishing societies
- Promote responsible aquaculture
- Community engagement in monitoring
- Repeat detailed goals per species & region (~40 lines)

------------------------------------------------------------
2. Regulatory Compliance
------------------------------------------------------------
- Enforce seasonal bans, size limits, geofencing
- Licensing: Mechanized & motorized vessels
- Daily reporting of catch & location
- Random inspections
- Penalties: First ₹50,000; repeated → license suspension + gear seizure
- GPS & logbook mandatory
- Auditing of reports monthly
- Training programs for fishers
- Repeat compliance examples for regions & species (~50 lines)

------------------------------------------------------------
3. Market & Trade Policies
------------------------------------------------------------
- Monitor mandi prices
- Encourage cooperative marketing
- Regulate export of aquaculture products
- Traceability to prevent IUU products
- Promote value-added processing
- Dynamic price advisory via AI forecasts
- Integration with policy dashboards
- Repeat examples & scenarios (~50 lines)

------------------------------------------------------------
4. Environmental Considerations
------------------------------------------------------------
- Protect marine sanctuaries & coral reefs
- Avoid low-oxygen zones
- Sustainable deep-sea fishing recommended
- Monitor monsoon & climate effects
- Restrict gear types harmful to ecosystem
- Periodic evaluation of environmental impact
- Repeat environmental guidance per region (~50 lines)

------------------------------------------------------------
5. Forecast & Advisory Integration
------------------------------------------------------------
- AI forecast guides fishing schedules & locations
- Alerts for high-risk zones
- Recommend profitable fishing spots
- Integrate mandi prices & fish abundance
- Daily & weekly advisory notes
- Repeat advisory examples per species (~50 lines)

------------------------------------------------------------
6. Safety Guidelines
------------------------------------------------------------
- Onboard safety procedures
- Communication with authorities
- Eco-friendly nets to reduce bycatch
- Logbooks & GPS tracking
- Life jackets & emergency protocols
- Training modules for small-scale fishers
- Repeat safety instructions (~50 lines)
